# Ethics and Compliance Guidelines

## Data Collection Principles

- Only collect data that is publicly available or explicitly authorized by the data owner.
- Respect platform terms of service, legal requirements, and local regulations during data acquisition.

## Personal Data Protection

- Do not store personally identifiable information (PII) in raw or derived datasets.
- Apply sanitization and field-level filtering before persistence or sharing.

## Research Scope and Purpose

- This project is strictly for observation, threat analysis, and defensive research purposes.
- Outputs should focus on understanding influence patterns and improving resilience against information operations.

## Prohibited Use Cases

- Do not use this project, its data, or derived insights for covert manipulation campaigns.
- Do not use findings to conduct deceptive dissemination, astroturfing, harassment, or coordinated inauthentic behavior.

## Data Retention and Access Recommendations

- Define and document a retention window (e.g., 90-180 days for raw ingested data) and remove expired records on schedule.
- Keep only minimally necessary processed artifacts for reproducibility and audit.
- Restrict access by least privilege (role-based access control) and log access to sensitive datasets.
- Encrypt data at rest and in transit where applicable.
- Periodically review retention and access policy compliance.
